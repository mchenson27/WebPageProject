About Me

My name is Matt Henson and I am from Forsyth County, GA and I am 22 years old. I have a twin sister, a younger sister, and an older brother. My parents names are Rob and Tracy Henson. I am a communications studies major here at UGA. My passions include Dawgs football and Young Life and playing guitar. I am currently a Young Life leader at Oconee County High School where I spend on average 20 hours a week hanging out with high schoolers. It is one of my favorite things to do because Young Life focuses on building relationships with kids who are sometimes neglected by their friends or families. Young Life's mission is to develop deep and meaningful relationships with kids to then share the Gospel with them and since being involved in high school, it has been a massive part of my life. After college I plan on working for Young Life full time.
